About me

Juan Zou, Ph.D., professor, doctoral supervisor, deputy dean of School of Computer Network, director of Hunan Engineering Research Centre for Intelligent System Optimization and Security, Fellow of RSA (Royal Society of Arts and Crafts), selected as one of the top 2% of the world's top scientists in the list of 2024 released by Stanford University, famous teacher of Furong in Hunan Province, executive director of Artificial Intelligence Society of Hunan Province, vice president of Hunan Provincial Specialized Committee of Computer Education, winner of the second prize of Hunan Natural Science (2024: rank 1), winner of the first prize of Hunan Provincial Teaching Achievement twice (2024: rank 1), winner of the first prize of Hunan Province Teaching Achievement. Vice President of Hunan Provincial Association of Artificial Intelligence, Head of National First Class Speciality of Software Engineering, Winner of the Second Prize of Natural Science in Hunan Province (2024: Rank 1), Winner of the First Prize of Teaching Achievements in Hunan Province (2022: Rank 1, 2019: Rank 3), Moderator of National First Class Curriculum ‘Algorithmic Design and Analysis’, Second Prize of National First Innovative Teaching Competition. She is the second prize winner of the National First Innovative Teaching Competition, the academic leader of Shaofeng Scholars, the scientific and technological specialists in three regions of Hunan Province, the scientific and technological specialists in Xiangtan City, the member of IEEE, the member of the Computer Society, the main research fields are artificial intelligence, intelligent optimisation algorithms, evolutionary algorithms.

Presided over 3 projects of the National Natural Science Foundation of China (NSFC), including 2 projects of NSFC Upper Level Fund, ‘Research on Multi-objective Evolutionary Optimisation Methods Based on Dynamic Preference Problems’ and ‘Research on Multi-objective Evolutionary Optimisation Methods Based on Dynamic Constraints’, National Natural Science Foundation of China Youth Fund, ‘Research on Multi-objective Evolutionary Methods Based on Dynamic Environment’; presided over a project of Provincial Science and Technology Department, ‘Research on Solving the Problem of Extracting the Eigenvalues of Network Textual Information by High-Dimensional Multi-objective Evolutionary Algorithms’; presided over a key project of Education Department, ‘Research on Multi-objective Evolutionary Optimization Methods Based on Dynamic Preference Problem’. One key project ‘Research on Dynamic Preference Algorithm Based on Reference Points’, and one general project ‘Text-based Eigenvalue Extraction’ of the Department of Education; presided over two teaching reform projects of the Ministry of Education, and two provincial teaching reform projects. She has participated in 4 projects of the National Natural Science Foundation of China, and 3 projects of provincial and ministerial level; he is a key member of the ‘Xiangtan University Teaching Management System’.

In the past 5 years, Science Press has published 1 academic monograph on ‘Multi-objective Evolutionary Optimisation’; more than 100 academic papers have been published in domestic authoritative journals and international SCI/EI source journals and conferences, of which 59 papers are SCI journals, and more than 40 SCI papers, including 34 SCI first-region papers and 3 ESI top 1% highly cited papers, have been published on Google Scholar in the past 5 years, and the average impact factor of SCI is 7.4/paper. She has published more than 40 SCI papers, including 34 papers in SCI region I, 3 highly cited papers in the top 1% of ESI, with an average impact factor of 7.4/paper in SCI; more than 2,200 citations in SCI in the last 5 years in Google Scholar; applied for 50 patents for invention as the first inventor, and 26 were authorised, among which 6 international patents were authorised (5 in the United States); and 8 teaching awards and scientific research awards at the provincial and ministerial levels have been granted to the first completer. She has received 8 provincial and ministerial level awards for teaching and scientific research.

Exchange visits in the United States of America from January 2016-March 2016, July 2016-September 2016, and January 2017-March 2017, respectively.Exchange visits in the United Kingdom from July-August 2017.Exchange visits in the United States of America from January 2016-March 2016, July 2016-September 2016, and January 2017-March 2017, respectively.
